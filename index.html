Newest Project

Topographic signatures of life.

I recently started this project during May of 2025. After finishing my ozone model, I wanted to find something to apply it to. Life, as we know it, would not exist without some combination of processes. This work is dedicated to isolating and defining these processes as they happen over the many eons of Earth's development.
